    Compact way to create lists.

    ğŸª„ 7. Zip

    Combine iterables.

    print(name, score)

    Unzip with *:

    zipped = list(zip(names, scores))
names, scores = zip(*zipped)

    âš™ï¸ 8. Inline Functions (Lambdas)

    Short anonymous functions.
